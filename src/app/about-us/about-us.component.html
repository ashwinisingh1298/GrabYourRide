About Us

Founded in 2022, GrabYourRide has pioneered car sharing across emerging markets. With its asset-light marketplace-focused business model, the GrabYourRide platform connects host vehicle owners with guests who choose from a selection of otherwise idle cars to use for personal, business and travel needs. With operations across India GrabYourRide is transforming the urban mobility landscape with the introduction of affordable, sustainable and convenient transportation solutions. GrabYourRide currently operates across more than 50 cities in India and plans to continue building smart, convenient and sustainable transport solutions for emerging market consumers.
